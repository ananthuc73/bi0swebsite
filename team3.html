About bi0s

Team bi0s from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a well-established group of computer security enthusiasts focusing on various areas like web application security, computer forensics, binary exploitation, reverse engineering, hardware security, cryptography and Android security. bi0s is largely a CTF team actively taking part in the international CTFs round the year besides carrying out research on security issues in the real world. Fuelled by the urge for community service, bi0s has been organizing workshops and educating the masses about the need for cyber security.
